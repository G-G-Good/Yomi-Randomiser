Fantasy Strike -based Lineup Generator

This is a tool (constructed by GGG) for choosing a lineup given a set of three Yomi/Puzzle Strike/Fantasy Strike characters and a predetermined seed for a best-of-five match using the Fantasy Strike best-of-five ranked mode system. Choose your three characters from the dropdown menu and a number from 1 to 12 (given by your tournament organiser, or hit "random" if you're using this casually), and enter for each match whether you win or lose.
